§ 3Rules baked into every asset

  • Lead with money and safety, not specs — peso savings, brownout hours, typhoon durability, fixed price. Watts and efficiency % stay below the fold (§3).
  • Past-tense hooks — "how long was your last brownout?", "bill up again?" — never "would you buy solar?" (Mom Test, §2).
  • Outcome next to the price, always. The "reason why" it's ~₱50k under installers rides on every ad (fixed package, standardized BOM, no showroom, no barge).
  • No backup claim on on-grid (LIWANAG); backup language only on ILAW/SANDIGAN (§1.6). No fabricated proof, ratings, or install counts — ever (§7).
  • Every link is UTM-tagged to Test 1 and lands on the calculator / lead form.
