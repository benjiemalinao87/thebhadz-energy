# Secretary's Certificate — Bank Account Opening

**Template only — not legal advice.** This is the document banks ask for when opening
the corporate account (checklist §3): a Corporate Secretary's certification of the
board resolution naming the bank and the authorized signatories. Most banks have their
own preferred wording — bring this, but expect the branch to hand you their form; the
content below is what you'll need to transcribe either way. Fill every `[BRACKETED]`
field and delete this notice before signing.

---

REPUBLIC OF THE PHILIPPINES )

[CITY/MUNICIPALITY OF VENUE] ) S.S.

## Certification

I, **[CORPORATE SECRETARY NAME]**, Filipino, of legal age, with office address at
[PRINCIPAL OFFICE ADDRESS], being the duly elected and qualified **Corporate
Secretary** of **[COMPANY NAME], INC.** (the "Corporation"), a corporation organized
and existing under Philippine law with SEC Registration No. [SEC REG NO.], do hereby
certify that:

1. At the meeting of the Board of Directors of the Corporation duly called and held on
[DATE] at [PLACE / "via remote communication"], at which meeting a quorum was present
and acting throughout, the following resolutions were unanimously adopted:

**RESOLVED**, that the Corporation open and maintain one or more deposit accounts with
**[BANK NAME], [BRANCH]** (the "Bank");

**RESOLVED FURTHER**, that the following officers are authorized signatories on the
Corporation's accounts with the Bank, any [SIGNING RULE, e.g., "one" / "two"] of whom
may sign checks, withdrawal slips, and instructions on behalf of the Corporation:

| Name | Position | Specimen signature |
|---|---|---|
| [SIGNATORY 1] | [President] | |
| [SIGNATORY 2] | [Treasurer] | |

**RESOLVED FINALLY**, that the Bank may rely on this authority until it receives a
written notice of revocation or amendment, certified by the Corporate Secretary.

2. The foregoing resolutions have not been amended, modified, or revoked and remain in
full force and effect as of the date of this Certificate.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand this [DAY] day of [MONTH], [YEAR], in
[CITY], Philippines.

**[CORPORATE SECRETARY NAME]**, Corporate Secretary — TIN / valid government ID no.:
[ID NUMBER]

**SUBSCRIBED AND SWORN** to before me this [DAY] day of [MONTH], [YEAR], in [CITY],
Philippines, affiant exhibiting to me the identification stated above.
