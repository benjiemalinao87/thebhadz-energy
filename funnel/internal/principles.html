Our Vision · where we're going

To be the Philippines' leading homegrown energy and engineering company.

Recognized for engineering excellence, innovation, and integrity — empowering homes, businesses, and communities with clean, efficient, and reliable power. We're building the Filipino-built energy company that drives national progress and puts light in every home.

Our Mission · what we do every day

We deliver reliable, sustainable energy and electrical-engineering solutions — high-quality solar power, electrical systems, and advanced energy technology — done right, priced honestly, and backed by a local team that stands behind the work.

For homes, businesses, and communities across the Philippines. We exceed expectations by being safe, efficient, and cost-effective — and by staying accountable for the life of every system. Trust, not the cheapest quote, is how this industry is won, and how we build toward a cleaner, more sustainable future.

Core Values

how we behave · 7

Not wall decoration — the behaviors we hire for, decide by, and hold each other to. Each one comes with what it actually looks like on a normal Tuesday.

Integrity

We conduct business with honesty, transparency, and accountability.

In practiceSay the hard thing to each other's face, never behind backs. Quote real numbers. If we're wrong, we own it.

Innovation

We embrace technology and continuously improve our solutions.

In practiceTry the better way, measure it, keep what works. Yesterday's method isn't sacred.

Excellence

We strive for the highest standard in every project we undertake.

In practiceIf our name is on it, it lasts. We'd rather lose a sale than ship something we'd be ashamed of.

Safety

We protect the well-being of our people, clients, and communities.

In practicePhilippine Electrical Code, every time. Typhoon-rated, no exceptions. Nobody gets hurt to hit a deadline.

Sustainability

We protect the environment through clean and renewable energy.

In practiceEvery install we complete is one less home on dirty, expensive power. That's the point, not a slogan.

Customer Commitment

We build lasting relationships through reliable solutions and outstanding service.

In practiceSize honestly, don't oversell, treat their money like our own. Honor the warranty — ship the single replacement panel.

Teamwork

We foster collaboration, respect, and continuous learning to achieve shared success.

In practiceCompany first, role second, ego last. Cover for each other; win or lose together, aligned 100%.
