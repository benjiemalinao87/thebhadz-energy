§ 0Decide the entity first

Entity choice drives every step below
QuestionAnswer drives
How many founders hold equity? 1 → One Person Corporation (OPC) eligible. 2+ → regular domestic stock corporation (min. 2 incorporators, no statutory minimum capital for most activities).
SEC corp vs. DTI sole-prop? DTI is faster/cheaper (~1 day, <₱5,000) but unlimited personal liability, no corporate bank account, and reads as less credible to homeowners and distribution utilities. Default: SEC corporation (or OPC if solo).
Any foreign equity? Check the Foreign Investment Negative List before filing — flag for a lawyer if applicable.
Action Log the entity type + founder list as a decision in ops/status.md §4 before starting eSPARC. Everything below assumes a regular domestic stock corporation unless you're solo (OPC).
