# Treasurer's Affidavit — [COMPANY NAME], Inc.

**Template only — not legal advice.** Under RA 11232 the treasurer's certification is
normally embedded in the eSPARC-generated Articles of Incorporation, so a separate
notarized affidavit may not be required at filing — but banks, and some SEC processing
routes, still ask for one. Prepare it, and use it wherever it's requested. Fill every
`[BRACKETED]` field and delete this notice before signing.

---

## Affidavit

I, **[TREASURER NAME]**, [NATIONALITY], of legal age, with residence at [ADDRESS],
after having been duly sworn in accordance with law, depose and state that:

1. I have been elected by the subscribers as **Treasurer** of **[COMPANY NAME], INC.**,
a corporation being organized under the laws of the Republic of the Philippines, with
principal office at [PRINCIPAL OFFICE ADDRESS], and I am authorized to receive for and
in the name of the corporation all subscriptions paid by the subscribers.

2. The authorized capital stock of the corporation is **₱[AUTHORIZED AMOUNT]**, divided
into [NUMBER OF SHARES] shares with a par value of ₱[PAR VALUE] per share.

3. Of the authorized capital stock, **₱[SUBSCRIBED AMOUNT]** has been subscribed, and
**₱[PAID-UP AMOUNT]** of the subscription has been paid and received by me in cash
[and/or property, describe if applicable], for the benefit and credit of the
corporation.

4. I undertake to keep the paid subscriptions in trust for the corporation and to
deposit them in the corporation's name upon the opening of its bank account.

[CITY], Philippines.
