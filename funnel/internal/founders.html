§ 1Why this page exists

Building takes longer than it looks from outside, and it rarely looks like a straight line. Every founder on this deck hit a point where the company could plausibly have ended — and the lesson survived even where the specific company almost didn't. Read one before a hard week, not instead of doing the work in front of you.
