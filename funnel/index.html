Frequently asked questions

How much can I really save?
It depends on your usage and roof. Send your bill and we'll estimate your specific monthly savings instead of giving a generic average.
How does net-metering work?
For grid-tied systems, excess daytime power can be credited through your distribution utility. We prepare the paperwork and coordinate the process.
Do I need to change my Meralco meter?
Grid-tied net-metering requires utility approval and meter coordination. We include that process in the project scope.
How long is the installation?
Most standard residential installs are completed in 1-2 days after design, approvals, and equipment are ready.
What warranties do you offer?
You get BADJJ workmanship support plus manufacturer warranties for panels, inverter, and battery components where applicable.
Can I add a battery later?
Yes, for systems designed with future expansion in mind. We will recommend the right architecture during your estimate.
